Key Insights
  • SMOTE oversampling significantly improved minority class (attack) detection rates across both models.
  • SVM with RBF kernel outperforms Random Forest by ~1.3% on accuracy and generalizes better on unseen traffic.
  • Feature engineering on the NSL-KDD dataset reduced dimensionality and improved convergence speed.
  • Class imbalance was the primary challenge; SMOTE resolved false-negative rates on rare attack classes.
